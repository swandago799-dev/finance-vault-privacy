# Privacy Policy – Finance Vault

**Last updated:** February 2026

Finance Vault is a personal finance tracking application designed to help users
record income, expenses, and view spending trends. Your privacy is important to us.

## Information Collection and Use
Finance Vault does not collect, transmit, or store personal data on external servers.
All financial data entered into the app is stored locally on the user’s device only.

## Data Sharing
The app does not share any data with third parties. No analytics services,
advertising networks, or cloud services are used.

## Data Security
All data remains on the user’s device. Users may delete all data by clearing
app data or uninstalling the app.

## Children’s Privacy
Finance Vault is intended for users aged 18 and over. The app does not knowingly
collect data from children.

## Financial Disclaimer
Finance Vault is a personal finance tracking tool only and does not provide
financial, legal, or investment advice.
